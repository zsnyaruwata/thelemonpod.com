About Us

Welcome to The Lemon Pod

The charming sight from the kitchen window of lemon trees lined all along the vegetable garden wall, the green and bright yellow colour of this juicy fruit, the fresh zesty scent blowing in the air; are all childhood memories we so cherish.

The Lemon Pod is an apt name for our accommodation as it encapsulates this happy childhood of a big diverse family where there was always so much joy and laughter. Cherished recollection of moments around enjoying a lemon cup of tea with family, watching and leaning from our Mom as she made her exquisite lemon chilli pickle, passing on the recipes of her diverse culture to the next generation in such a loving way, and off course the many special aromas of the most delicious recipes created around those family moments that made us all lick our lips in anticipation for the flavours.

This is THE LEMON POD which we wish to share with you.

We invite you to come and enjoy that with us, from our cozy surrounds, to our organic greens from our garden.

You will want to make this YOUR NEXT SPACE!!!!
